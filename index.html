Goalz Restaurant Group

Goalz LLC is a priemere restaurant fast food chains(Church's Chicken, Dog Haus, Captain D's) throughout the US. Here, I created a custom WordPress theme for Goalz so they could update there content, add a new pin on google maps, or create blog/media posts all through the WP admin.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, PHP, WordPress, Underscores theme, ACF, ACPT.

Tech Talent Pipeline

TTP is an initiative by mayor de Blasio to deliver tech jobs to New Yorker's. This project was a campaign shown on NYC subways and buses to promote their programs and get people involved. Over 1,000 New Yorker's signed up to learn about "tech types" and where to get started.

Technologies used: Sketch, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, SquareSpace.

Spire Energy

Spire Energy is a mid-west company providing energy to thousands of American homes. For there 2017 annual report they wanted to go digital instead of there typical PDF format. We created a year in review HTML5 micro-site and pushed the code to their current site under a sub-directory.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, jQuery, Greensock, vanilla JavaScript.
